Calm clinical confidence

AA's crimson carries the identity, a deep-teal accent does the work, quiet neutrals and six status colours do the semantic lifting. Chrome stays calm; delight lives in the actions — completing a card, watching the fee tick, submitting a list. Reference for all three apps: mobile, anaesthetist web, admin.
